moonlight website concept

My Role: UI Designer; Type of Project: Personal; Software: Figma

"Moonlight" is my favorite movie because of its nuanced storytelling, and mesmerizing cinematography and score.




The concept was to create a website that would introduce the user to the movie "Moonlight". The website would share a summary of the movie made by A24, the cast, the trailer, the awards and acclaim, and where to watch the movie. I owned none of the images, descriptions, or quotes.
