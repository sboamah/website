moonlight website concept

My Role: UI Designer; Type of Project: Personal; Software: Figma


"Moonlight" is my favorite movie because of its nuanced storytelling, and mesmerizing cinematography and score.



The concept was to create a website that would introduce the user to the movie "Moonlight". The website would share a summary of the movie made by A24, the cast, the trailer, the awards and acclaim, and where to watch the movie. I own none of the images, descriptions, or quotes. Website for project coming soon!


Colors and Typography


The colors were inspired by the Moonlight poster and the cool hues in the cinematography.
